Why We Started

A Legacy of Flavor

It began in our grandmother's kitchen in Kerala, where the aroma of freshly roasted spices filled the air. For three generations, we've perfected the art of blending — sourcing the finest turmeric from Tamil Nadu, cardamom from Kerala, and chillies from Andhra Pradesh.

Today, MasalaBox Co. brings that same farm-to-kitchen promise to your home. Every blend is handcrafted, every ingredient traceable, and every box packed with love.

About MasalaBox Co.

🌿

MasalaBox Co. was born from a simple belief: that the world deserves to taste Indian spices the way they were meant to be — pure, fresh, and full of soul.

We work directly with over 200 smallholder farmers across India, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices. Every spice is sun-dried, stone-ground, and blended by hand in small batches.

From our family to yours — welcome to the MasalaBox Co. family.
